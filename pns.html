PERMIAS NASIONAL CUP II

GO BIG OR GO HOME

02/15/2016

Seattle, WA

ABOUT

PERMIAS NASIONAL CUP II is an annual double-event sports tournament held by PERMIAS National inviting champions from each PERMIAS chapter across the United States of America. We will host both basketball and futsal competitions in this event.

This year we will have a collaborative event of PERMIAS Seattle and the Indonesian Student Association at the University of Washington (ISAUW) to bring a new concept of PERMIAS NASIONAL CUP in Seattle.
